Hydrythm

A wearable air purification helmet that uses only water — no disposable filters ever — to create a personal clean-air bubble in polluted environments. Clean air shouldn't be a luxury.

Visit Project ↗

How It Works — 4 Stage Process

  1. 1Polluted air enters through side intake grills with a coarse pre-filter mesh — the first barrier against large particles.
  2. 2Two small centrifugal micro-blowers (adjustable speed, 20–40 L/min) pull air inside at controlled rates.
  3. 3Air passes through a transparent water cartridge (30–50 ml). PM2.5, PM10, dust, pollen and gases get trapped. You can literally see pollution collecting in the water.
  4. 4Clean air delivers directly into a sealed zone around your face. Excess air vents safely downward.

What Makes It Different

Fully open-source — complete materials list, 3D-print files (PETG/TPU), and assembly instructions. No filter replacements. Ever. Just rinse and refill.

Optional V2 adds a PM2.5 sensor with real-time AQI display. Designed in Dubai with Delhi-level AQI in mind — for everyone, not just those who can afford it.
