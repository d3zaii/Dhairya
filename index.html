"Since a very young age, I have consistently pushed myself to learn and create — from growing a YouTube channel to 420,000+ views, to composing music, to building Dezai AI."

Dhairya Desai is a senior student at GEMS New Millennium School, Dubai — studying Physics, Biology and Chemistry — while simultaneously founding and running an AI-powered education platform. His journey spans content creation, music production, web development, an aviation internship at Sarsan Aviation Academy, and youth leadership across 10+ Model United Nations conferences.

He founded Dezai AI to democratise access to intelligent study tools — removing paywalls, sign-in barriers, and complexity so that any student, anywhere, can benefit. The platform integrates multiple AI models for refined accuracy and has attracted active users across India and the UAE.
